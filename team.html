Paul, Creative Director / Producer

Paul Baker is an Game Industry veteran with 26 years experience in various roles, mostly design. Paul has worked on 26 published games since 1991.


Jamie, Project Lead

Jamie Cushenan is an Irish university student who took to programming fairly early in his life and has since learned an array of programming languages. In 2015, he started a small game development team, Bundle of Sticks Studios (BOSS), and has since worked with his team as lead developer on a number of projects.


Gil, Artist

Gil is a illustrator and concept designer living in Mexico city who likes creating art, and playing video games. He previously worked as a graphic and web designer in the advertising industry but left it to pursue the videogame and art industries.


Lucas, Writer

Lucas got involved with BOSS feeling the need to develop out the world, he has since used his passion as a driving force to develop objectives and written insight to the galaxy.


Waffles, Sound Design

Waffles From Nowhere is a full time breakfast food musician and part time Youtuber. He has been working with BOSS since June 2015 and currently handles both the Audio and Trailer Video production.


Ian, Social Media Manager

Content Creator, Gamer, Writer. Ian Berendsen has a number of hobbies and skills including his own YouTube channel, Shikibara, where he makes videos of gaming news, reviews, and skits. He was brought onto BOSS in late July of 2016 as their Social Media Manager.


Daniel, 3D Modeler

Daniel Chavez is a professional who worked on movies and tv shows, during his free time, he likes to learn and stay up to date on new softwares. He moved back to Switzerland from the USA in 2015 and had to join the freelance and indie scene to continue working in the CG industry.


Suhail, Progammer

Suhail is a programmer who is currently working with BOSS. Prior to joining BOSS, Suhail has done some indie projects and is passionate about innovations in coding and gaming applications, he has graduated from the Kashmir University with a B.Sc. in Computer Science.


Gaby, Web Developer

Gaby Justice is a Web Designer/Developer currently working toward her Computer Science degree at Indiana University-Purdue University Indianapolis. She primarily works on developing and maintaining all websites for BOSS.
